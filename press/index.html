Fraunhofer Portugal Challenge 2020 Results

News items featuring the Fraunhofer Challenge results. While mostly focused on the 1st prize, some also feature my PhD work, which received the 2nd prize at the contest.

28 October 2020

90 Segundos de Ciência - Ep. 824 Rui Pedro Paiva

Professor Rui Pedro Paiva presented the MOODetector project in the 90 segundos de ciência (90 seconds of science) radio program (Antena 1).

02 April 2020

Os Dias do Futuro - Software que reconhece emoções na música desenvolvido na UC

Professor Rui Pedro Paiva, my Ph.D. supervisor and coordinator of the MOODetector project gave a long radio interview where our work in MER, including my Ph.D. thesis, was referenced. Starts at 20:22.

20 July 2019

TSF Interview - Que tipo de emoção nos transmite uma determinada música?

I was interviewed in the TSF national radio about our work in the field of Music Emotion Recognition using both audio and lyrics.

10 July 2019

Programa informático que avalia a emoção presente nas canções

The Press Officer of the University of Coimbra interviewed us regarding the recent advances in our work, especially with the completion of my Ph.D. focusing on audio, and Ricardo Malheiro’s Ph.D. focused on lyrics. The information was published in several national newspapers.

08 July 2019

RUC Interview - Software capaz de identificar emoções em peças musicais

Professor Rui Pedro Paiva participated in a radio program about our work on music emotion recognition using audio and lyrics.

08 July 2019

República do Saber S4E59 - MOODetector Project

Professor Rui Pedro Paiva, my Ph.D. supervisor at the time, was interviewed in the scope of the MOODetector project to the science TV show "República do Saber", aired in RTP2. It starts at 9:27, I make a brief explanation of the prototype which can be seen at 11:30.

20 December 2012

SÁBADO - Figura do Futuro

I was mentioned as a "figure of the future" by the SÁBADO magazine. This was a weekly column where the magazine interviewed young people who stood up, in my case it was due to the news about the MOODetector project and MIREX contest.

Universo UC - MOODetector Project & Prototype
